Soy-and-Ginger-Glazed Salmon with Udon Noodles

  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce, plus 2 tablespoons extra
  • 1 tablespoon mirin, plus 1 tablespoon extra
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 350 gram skinless salmon fillet, cut into 2 cm cubes
  • 375 gram packet udon noodles
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon pickled ginger, finely sliced, plus 1 tablespoon pickling liquid
  • 1 bunch broccolini, chopped, steamed
